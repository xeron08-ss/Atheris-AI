🧠 Deepfake Awareness Hub

Understanding the deepfake landscape — how AI-generated media works and how to spot it.

👁️
Visual Artifacts
Look for unnatural skin smoothness, ear inconsistencies, hair boundary blurring, and lighting mismatches.
🎭
Face Swap Tells
GAN-generated faces often show inconsistent blinking, asymmetric eye specular reflections, and edge artifacts.
🌊
Diffusion Patterns
Stable Diffusion and DALL·E images show characteristic noise patterns detectable in frequency domain.
🎙️
Voice Cloning
AI-cloned voices lack natural breath patterns, show compression artifacts, and have unnatural prosody.
📱
Social Media Risk
AI-generated content spreads 6× faster than corrections. Always verify before sharing sensitive media.

Frequently Asked Questions
What exactly does Atheris AI detect? ▾
Atheris detects AI-generated images, deepfake videos, voice-cloned audio, and AI-written text. It analyzes metadata, pixel noise patterns, GAN fingerprints, facial geometry, and frequency-domain signals to identify synthetic media with 98.4% accuracy.
Is my file uploaded to any server? ▾
Absolutely not. Every analysis happens 100% locally in your browser using WebAssembly-compiled models. Your files never leave your device. No servers, no uploads, no data collection — ever. This is a core promise of Atheris AI.
What file formats are supported? ▾
Images: JPG, PNG, WEBP. Videos: MP4, MOV, WEBM. Audio: MP3, WAV, M4A, OGG, FLAC. Text: paste any text directly. Batch mode supports up to 20 files at once.
How do I interpret my AI Score? ▾
0–30%: Very likely authentic. 31–49%: Probably authentic with minor uncertainty. 50–69%: Mixed signals — treat with caution. 70–85%: Likely AI-generated. 86–100%: Almost certainly AI-generated. Always combine the score with Confidence % for the full picture.
What is the Confidence score? ▾
The Confidence score tells you how certain Atheris is about its AI Score. A 95% AI score with 90% confidence is very reliable. A 70% AI score with 55% confidence means the result is less certain and you should seek additional verification.
Can Atheris detect ChatGPT or Claude-written text? ▾
Yes. Atheris uses perplexity analysis, burstiness scoring, stylometric fingerprinting, and transformer attention pattern matching to detect text from GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, Llama, Mistral, and other major LLMs with ~93% accuracy.
What does the heatmap show? ▾
The heatmap overlay highlights which regions of your image triggered AI artifact detections. Red areas have the highest suspicion (GAN artifacts, unnatural textures), yellow is moderate, and blue is low suspicion. Toggle it on after running an analysis.
Is Atheris free to use? ▾
Yes — the core detection features are completely free with no limits. Enterprise plans add team workspaces, API access, custom model training, and compliance reporting for organizations needing to scale their verification pipeline.
Why does my result say "Uncertain"? ▾
Uncertainty happens when the AI score falls in the 40–60% range and/or confidence is below 70%. This can occur with highly edited real photos, partially humanized AI text, or very new AI models not yet in our database. Try running in Deep Neural mode for a more thorough scan.
